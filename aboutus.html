Who we are and What we do

Program Bots is a super simple, fun and creative place for kids to learn real world skills to attune themselves and fit right in to the new world, their world! A world where the technology is the tool. Mobile computing and Internet of Things are changing the face of the world as we know it. Kids are already consuming technology faster than we can imagine! They are also destined to be the creators of many disruptive and phenomenal ideas that will shape the future to come.

Program Bots takes an initiative in sowing the seeds of logic, creativity and innovation in kids at an early age. We aim to make the computing and robotics sessions purposeful and very easy so that the kids can derive maximum sense of achievement and also make their parents proud.

  • Stimulate the left and the right brains, creativity and logic
  • Build and learn philosophy for thorough understanding
  • Simple, step by step instructions for rapid learning
  • Introduction to programming and robotics at an early age
  • A unique blend of Computer, Mobile and Robot programming

The Faculty

Our teachers have a penchant for teaching kids in the simplest and most effective ways. Together the teachers bring over 14 years of experience in Computing, Internet of Things and Robotics.
